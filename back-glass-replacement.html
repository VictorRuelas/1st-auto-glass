Back Glass Replacement

Expert Rear Window Replacement in Austin

Unlike a windshield that typically cracks when hit, your vehicle's back glass is usually made of tempered glass. When broken, it completely shatters into hundreds of tiny pieces. At 1st Auto Glass, our technicians are trained to effectively replace back glass on any make and model across Austin, TX.

Our Process

  • Inspection & Damage Control: We assess the damage and identify if defrost lines or integrated antennas are present.
  • Extensive Cleanup: We meticulously vacuum all shattered glass inside your vehicle, the trunk compartment, and within the door or hatch panels.
  • Precision Replacement: We prime and install a brand new, OEM-quality back glass using industry-leading urethane.
  • System Reconnection: We reconnect defrost units and integrated technologies to ensure full functionality.
