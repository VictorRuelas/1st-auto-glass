Rock Chip Repair

Stop Cracks Before They Spread

A small rock chip can quickly turn into a large crack requiring a full windshield replacement. Fortunately, if caught early, 1st Auto Glass can repair small chips restoring the structural integrity of your windshield and improving optical clarity. Our mobile service makes rock chip repair in Austin, TX easier than ever.

Our Process

  • Damage Assessment: We evaluate the chip's size and depth to ensure it qualifies for a safe repair.
  • Cleaning & Preparation: We clear out microscopic debris and moisture from inside the chip.
  • Resin Injection: Using a specialized vacuum pressure device, we inject an optically matched, high-quality resin into the void.
  • Curing & Polishing: We cure the resin with UV light and polish the surface so the wipers glide smoothly over it.
